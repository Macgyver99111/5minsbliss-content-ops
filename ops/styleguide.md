# Style Guide (v1)

## Brand voice
- Reflective, calm, empowering
- Story-led, practical ending
- Keep symbolic metaphor + actionable steps

## Article skeleton
1. Opening affirmation
2. Symbol/story framing
3. Why this matters now
4. Practical framework (3-5 steps)
5. 4-week plan / practice prompts
6. CTA + brand closing
