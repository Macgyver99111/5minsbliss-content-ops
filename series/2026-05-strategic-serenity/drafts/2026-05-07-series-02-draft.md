# Read the Momentum — Don’t Fight the River

“I move with timing, not panic. I respect momentum, then place my effort where it multiplies.”

Zizhi Tongjian is full of one recurring lesson: leaders fail when they misread momentum.
Creators do the same — publishing randomly, reacting emotionally, and confusing activity with traction.

Dan Koe’s approach reminds us: direction + consistency + feedback = compounding.
Ancient statecraft adds one more: timing.

## 3-Layer Momentum Board
1. **Signal Layer** — What is changing in audience demand?
2. **Action Layer** — What are this week’s 3 irreversible actions?
3. **Adjustment Layer** — What did data invalidate this week?

## 14-Day Cadence
- Mon: Publish flagship idea
- Tue: Capture responses
- Wed: Build derivative asset (carousel/video)
- Thu: Publish secondary perspective
- Fri: Synthesize and document learnings
- Sat: Deep work block
- Sun: Reset and reallocate focus

Momentum is not luck. It is disciplined listening.
